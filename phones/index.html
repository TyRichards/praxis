Best Phones for Digital Minimalists

Choosing the right phone is foundational to digital minimalism. The ideal device offers essential functionality without constant distraction. Consider phones with grayscale displays, limited apps, or intentional design that discourages endless scrolling. Physical keyboards, e-ink screens, and simplified operating systems can transform your relationship with technology. The goal isn't deprivation but intentionality—selecting tools that serve your values rather than exploiting your attention.
